--- layout: page title: About me excerpt: "Jade Proulx is a Masters Student in Food Science at Cornell University." modified: 2014-08-08T19:44:38.564948-04:00 image: feature: jade-wide-10.jpg credit: Alex Litoiu creditlink: thumb: jade-logo.png --- I am a Masters student in Food Science at Cornell University working under the supervision of Dr. Carmen Moraru. My current work involves developing novel strategies for the surface decontamination of foods using non-thermal technologies. I have have previously interned in both R&D and QC at two different flavour companies and I am HACCP-certified. I have also been involved in numerous food-related entrepreneurial projects such as co-founding an exclusive restaurant recommendation app, organizing a Gastronomy & Science Symposium, and participating to food product development competitions. My interests lie at the interface between technology, food, sustainability, and entrepreneurship.
