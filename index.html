--- layout: page title: About me excerpt: "Jade Proulx is a Masters Student in Food Science at Cornell University." modified: 2014-08-08T19:44:38.564948-04:00 image: feature: jade-wide-10.jpg credit: Alex Litoiu creditlink: thumb: jade-logo.png --- I am a Masters student in Food Science at Cornell University. My current work involves developing novel strategies for the surface decontamination of foods using non-thermal technologies. I have work experience in the food industry, having previously interned in both R&D and QC at two different flavour companies. I have also been involved in numerous food-related entrepreneurial projects such as co-founding an exclusive restaurant recommendation app, organizing a Gastronomy & Science symposium, and participating to food product development competitions. My interests lie at the interface between technology, food, sustainability, and entrepreneurship.
